Blockchain & NFt

Web3 is better understood when we compare it Web2, which is the version of internet that we use today full of websites, intermediaries, and giant tech companies. Exploitation and centralization of user data for “monetization” is the key for Web2. Most of the web traffic is controlled by major tech companies and we -as consumers- enjoy the Web2 by giving and sharing our personal data to these companies.

Empowered by peer-to-peer protocols and blockchain technologies, Web3 comes with a few great things that Web2 does not have. Some of the main characteristics of Web3 can be listed as follows: decentralized, verifiable, permissionless, trustless, distributed, robust & having native built-in payments. All these technologies have the potential of transforming how we live, work, spend money, entertain, and socialize.

In Games United, we believe in decentralization and think company and user communities as a whole while focusing on creating digital applications that everyone benefits. The richness in our ecosystem lies within the young, diverse, and energetic talent pool in our partner studios.

In such a case, it would be impossible to reject any NFT-based blockchain game idea. So, our first ever blockchain game “Roboclash” was born with such a mindset. RoboClash is an NFT-based game with a Play-To-Earn structure where you can earn coins and in-game rewards by fighting in Daily Missions and PVP. Roboclash is also our first joint-venture business development with a USA-based fund. The NFT minting and the official game launch will be simultaneously very soon. Check us further at:
