Our Story

We are a Venture Builder which enables startup teams with capital, vision, and strategic direction. Unlike gaming VC’s, a venture builder deals with the day-to-day operations and strategic decisions to grow the new business.

We dont just provide the core capital, we also organize team building, game design, production, and publisher relations. Additionally, we support our studios in cashflow management, bookkeeping, legal affairs, and protection of IP.

We are a family consisting of 10 companies, more than 80 people who share, collaborate, and cheer together!
